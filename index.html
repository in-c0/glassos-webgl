GlassOS

A digital embryo whose signs are alive. Each one is a small genome of light — nothing here is drawn by hand. Your attention is their food: the forms you linger on survive and breed; the rest starve. Leave it alone, and it dreams new forms to show you when you return. It remembers, across visits. And it is also a desk: press ⌘K and conjure surfaces of glass over the field where it lives.
